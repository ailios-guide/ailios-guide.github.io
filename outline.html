Veryn - Home of Humans & Industry

This is the second largest continent of Aiolios and the center of all modern industry in Ailios. The continent features mountainous regions to the North and East with temperate weather, vast forests with trees of red hue, snow-peaked mountains and ridges. To the north of the continent lies the Lion Ocean off the cold shores. This region is home to the Esoterran Empire. Their main cities include, Estergate (Capitol city), Fairport (main harbor hub for the empire), Woodsmark (largest industrial city).

Off to the south the ridges falter into marshlands and the land is divided by many rivers providing transport to the Rai Ocean which lines the southern shores of Veryn.This land is made up of smaller settlements belonging to the Marinion Union. The citizens of the union have denounced the Esoterran Empire and live their lives according to their own rules and law. They have no shared economy or industry and all work for the betterment of the Union and the citizens of their settlement. Their major cities include Scarlet (Capitol City, large port settlement) and Trist Keep(Garrison City to protect the Union)

Quick Notes:

  • Home to the Esoterran Empire & Marinion Union
    • Esoterran Empire ruled by King Rorstam Esot
      • Established around ~300AC by Erin Esot
      • Controls the Northern and Eastern regions of Veryn
    • Marinion Union
      • Split off from the Esoterran Empire around 850AC
      • Controls the Southern trading ports and flatland regions
  • Major Cities
    • Estergate
      • Nested in the frosted hills of the Amberpine Ridge
      • First Human settlement created after the Deities relinquished the world to their creations
      • Home of universities teaching magics, industry and warfare.
    • Scarlet
      • Named after the southern reach of the continent The Scarlet Shore
      • Large settlement, no real castle/hold but vast homesteads and businesses for the large number of citizens
